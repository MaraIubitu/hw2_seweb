- **Hover**: Scales 1.15 + Rotates 10°

- **Gradient**: Pink (#FFB6D9) → Purple (#D4B5F3)

### 🔘 Button Ripple Effect
- **Trigger**: Click any button
- **Effect**: White circular wave spreads from center
- **Duration**: 0.6s smooth expansion
- **Feel**: Satisfying, interactive feedback

### 💫 Card Hover Lift
- **Trigger**: Hover over cards
- **Effect**: Lifts up 12px, scales 1.02

- **Duration**: 0.3s smooth transition
- **Feel**: Playful, inviting

- **Duration**: 0.3s smooth transition
- **Feel**: Responsive, feedback on action

### 💬 Widget Slide-Up
- **Trigger**: Click chatbot button

- **Duration**: 0.4s elastic bounce
- **Feel**: Snappy, responsive, delightful
